My Story

I was an insurance services, administrator and claims adjuster for 20 years so I've used computers but I've always wanted to work with computers. For so long I was unable to change careers due to resource and logistical hurdles plus the time it would take to get a degree part-time. I found out that coding bootcamps were a thing and saw it as an opportunity to get into the field that I desired to be in all along. So I quit my job to fully devote my time and effort to becoming a Full-Stack Web Developer and I enrolled in and completed the coding bootcamp at The University of North Carolina, Charlotte. I am open to coding in any capacity and I feel I would be most valuable leveraging my experience as a corporate computer user to serve in the technical support space on the front or back end.

Michael Neal
